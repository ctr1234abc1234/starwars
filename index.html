STAR WARS, one of the most inspirational series of movies of all-time, is a soap-opera, a sci-fi adventure, and a comedy all rolled into one. Created by George Lucas, of LucasFilm, in May of 1977, Star Wars easily became a fan favorite for a varierty of generations grossing $775.4 million in the box office. Following "Star Wars" (or, commonly known as "A New Hope"), Lucas and his team released "The Empire Strikes Back" and "Return of the Jedi" in 1980 and 1983, respectively. "The Empire Strikes Back" made around $535 million in box office while "Return of the Jedi" made $572 million.

In 1999, Lucasfilm shocked the world by releasing the movie "The Phantom Menace"; starting the new trilogy explaining how Darth Vater came to be. Captivating audiences around the globe, "Attack of the Clones" and "Return of the Jedi" were released in 2002 and 2005, respectively. "The Phantom Menace", "Attack of the Clones", and "Revenge of the Sith" grossed $1.027 billion, $649.4 million, and $848.8 million, respectively.

In 2012, the Walt Disney company, better known as Disney, aquired Lucasfilm for over $4 billion. Several life-time Star Wars fans were outraged by the event; however, Disney was not to disappoint. At that time, Disney announced three additional Star Wars films coming out in 2015, 2017, and 2019. Around Christmas of 2015, "The Force Awakens" was released grossing $689.4 million in just the first week.
